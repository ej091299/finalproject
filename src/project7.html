The Rise of Youtube

Authored By: Eric Johnson

YouTube is an American online video-sharing platform headquartered in San Bruno, California. YouTube allows users to upload, view, rate, share, add to playlists, report, comment on videos, and subscribe to other users.

2010 - Youtube starts off in 4th place for the most viewed website with about 3.5 billion monthly viewers, but by the end of 2010 they jumped up to 3rd with about 6 billion

2011 - By the end of 2011, early 2012, Facebook and Youtube both surpassed Yahoo a long time leader for the most visitors

2012 - By the end of 2012 Youtube is the 3rd most visited website with just about 12.5 billion monthly visitors just under Facebook

2015 - From 2012-2015 Youtube grew at a very fast rate jumping to 20 billion viewers coming in very close with Google and Facebook for the top spots but remained in 3rd
