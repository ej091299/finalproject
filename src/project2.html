The Rise of Yahoo

Authored By: Eric Johnson

Yahoo was one of the pioneers of the early Internet era in the 1990s. It provides a Web portal, search engine Yahoo! Search, and related services, including Yahoo! Directory, Yahoo! Mail, Yahoo! News, Yahoo! Finance, Yahoo! Groups, Yahoo! Answers, advertising, online mapping, video sharing, fantasy sports, and its social media website.

2000 - While companies like AOL and MSN are increasing visits, Yahoo increased at an exponential rate from the year prior almost beating AOL with 600 million monthly visits

2001- In 2001, Yahoo finally surpassed AOL in visits and was the first website to reach 1 billion monthly visitors

2002 - By the end of 2002 Yahoo had billions of more monthly visitors than any other site. Ending the year with close to 3.5 billion monthly visitors.

2003 - By the end of 2003 Yahoo had 5 billion monthly viewers. Google has just started uprising to about 1 billion visitors.
