The Start of Facebook

Authored By: Eric Johnson

Facebook is an American online social media and social networking service based in Menlo Park, California, and a flagship service of the namesake company Facebook, Inc. Facebook started in 2004 and grew greatly over the years to become a top social media platform that now owns other social media platforms as well.

2010 - Facebook currently in 3rd for the most viewed website with about 4.5 billion monthly viewers

2011 - By the end of 2011, early 2012, Facebook and Youtube both surpassed Yahoo a long time leader for the most visitors

2012 - By the end of 2012 Facebook is the 2nd most visited website with 13 billions monthly visitors

2015 - From 2012-2015 Facebook grew at a very fast rate jumping to 22 billion viewers coming in very close with Google for the top spot
