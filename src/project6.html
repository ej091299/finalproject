Google Back on Top

Authored By: Eric Johnson

2010 - At the start of 2010 Google was in 2nd behind Yahoo for most monthly visitors at a little over 9 billion. By the end of 2010, Google surpassed Yahoo with about 13.5 billion

2011 - While Yahoo was on the decline in monthly visitors, Google was increasingly becoming the first website to his 15 billion monthly viewers.

2013 - 2013 saw Google hit 20 billion visitors while Facebook and Youtube also grew coming closer to the top spot

2015 - 2015 is where Google really increased viewership, jumping up to 33 billion monthly viewers and only going up
