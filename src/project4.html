Yahoo Reclaims the Throne

Authored By: Eric Johnson

2008 - Yahoo takes back the number 1 spot as the most visited website with over 9 billion visitors, while Google is still close to 9 billion but not there yet.

2009 - Yahoo is the first website to reach over 10 million visitors, they finish the year with over 11 billion

2009 - Facebook and Youtube are rising in the 3rd and 4th place spots with over 3 billion monthly visitors
