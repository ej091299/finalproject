Google Joins the Race

Authored By: Eric Johnson

Google LLC is an American multinational technology company that specializes in Internet-related services and products, which include online advertising technologies, a search engine, cloud computing, software, and hardware.

2004 - By the end of 2004 to early 2005 Google was already the second most viewed site behind Yahoo with just over 2 billion monthly viewers. Yahoo had about 6 billion.

2005 - Google grew at an incredible amount in 2005 by going from 1 billion visitors to over 4 billion. Coming up close on Yahoo.

2006 - 2006 Google continues to grow and it surpasses Yahoo as the most visited website with over 7 billion monthly visitors. Yahoo drops down to about 5 billion.

2007 - Google remains at the top with close to 8 billion visitors. However, Yahoo is rising back with close to 7 billion themselves.
