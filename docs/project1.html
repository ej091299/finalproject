AOL Leads From the Beginning

Authored By: Eric Johnson

AOL was one of the early pioneers of the Internet in the mid-1990s, and the most recognized brand on the web in the United States. It originally provided a dial-up service to millions of Americans, as well as providing a web portal, e-mail, instant messaging and later a web browser

1996 - AOL starts as the most popular website with over 100,000,000 monthly visits

1997 - AOL continues to lead the pack reaching over 250,000,000 monthly visits, Yahoo is currently a growing site first hitting 100,000,000 visits

1998 - By the end of 1998 AOL has 500,000,000 visits, MSN surpasses Yahoo at number 2 with over 175,000,000 visits

1999 - The end of the Millenium AOL has over 750 million visits, while MSN and Yahoo have less than half of that
