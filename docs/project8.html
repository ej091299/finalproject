Google's Domination

Authored By: Eric Johnson

2016 - In 2016 Google just ran away with the lead for the most viewers finishing the year with 48 billion monthly visits, Facebook and Youtube had about half of that

2017 - The beginning of 2017 Google hit 50 billion visitors, but by the end they had reached 60 billion

2018 - More of the same for Google in 2018 gaining another 10 million monthly visitors for a total of 70 billion. 40 billion over any other site.

2019 - By the end of 2019, Google finished with 77 billion monthly visitors, 50 billion over Youtube who finished the year in second with 27 billion. Facebook finished in 3rd with 23.5 billion monthly viewers.
